How to make e-learning content interesting

The onus is on us to create engaging content for e-learning subjects. To do this we have a trick up our sleeve.



Use of colour

There is no colour rulebook for e-learning, unless they stipulate we must follow brand guidelines… but let's forget about that for now. We want to use colours that evoke emotions and encourage users to learn. When you know how the human mind perceives specific colours, you can use the power of colours to create the right mood and influence users. Welcome the psychology of colours…

We recommend using cool colours to help learners focus on the learning and improve the learning outcome.
Use warm colours if you want users to get excited or to inject some liveliness into a dull subject.
